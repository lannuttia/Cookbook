Chocolate Chip Cookies 36 Cookies

____________________________________________________________________________________________

30 minutes

1

Preheat oven to 375 ∘F. Line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.

2

3 Cups All-Purpose Flour

3

1 tsp Baking Soda

4

1/2 tsp Baking Powder

5

1 tsp Sea Salt

In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

6

1 Cup Softened Salted Butter

7

1 Cup Granulated Sugar

8

1 Cup Packed Light Brown

Cream together butter and sugars until combined.

9

2 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract

10

2 Large Eggs

Beat in eggs and vanilla extract until light. This will likely take about 1 minute. After this, start mixing in the dry ingredients until combined.

11

2 Cups Chocolate Chips

Add chocolate chips and mix well. Roll 2 – 3 tbsp balls of dough, flatten slightly and then place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8 – 10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown. Let them sit on the baking pan for 2 minutes before removing to cooling rack.

Coconut Cream Pie

____________________________________________________________________________________________

1

2

1 Pie Crust

Prepare Pie Crust.

3

1 1/2 Cups Coconut Milk

4

1 1/2 Cups Half and Half

Pour coconut milk and half and half into a liquid measuring cup.

5

5 Egg Yolks

Add egg yolks in measuring cup and whisk together. Set aside.

6

3/4 Cup Sugar

7

4 tbsp Cornstarch

Add sugar and cornstarch to a heavy bottomed saucepan over medium-low heat. Whisk egg and milk mix together again and slowly begin to add to sugar and corn starch, whisking together constantly. Bring the custard mix to a boil. Stir consistantly and switch to a rubber or wooden spatula. Boil for 1 minute. Remove from heat.

8

1 tbsp Butter

9

1 1/2 Cups Flaked Sweetened Coconut

10

1 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract

11

1/4 tsp Salt

Add butter, coconut, vanilla, and salt to custard mix. Spread custard into pie crust. Cover lightly with plastic wrap and chill in fridge until it sets.

12

Whipped Cream

Spread whipped cream on top of coconut custard. Refrigerate until it is ready to serve.

Dinner Rolls 24 Portions

____________________________________________________________________________________________

45 Minutes

1

Preheat oven to 400∘ F.

2

4 Cups Flour

3

1 Packet Instant Yeast

4

1/3 Cup Sugar

5

1 tsp Salt

6

1 1/2 Cups Warm Milk

7

5 tbsp Melted Butter

8

1 Egg

Combine flour, yeast, sugar, salt, warm milk, butter, and egg in a bowl. As you mix, incorporate 1/2 cup of flour until ball of dough is formed. Dough needs to be slightly sticky and soft. Put dough in greased bowl and cover with towel for 30 minutes at room temperature. Remove towel and deflate dough lightly. Punch it. Put 2 Ounce balls of dough on greased pan. Put in oven for 12–15 minutes or until golden brown.

French Bread 32 Slices

____________________________________________________________________________________________

1

2

2 Cups Warm Water

3

1 tbsp Active Dry Yeast

4

2 tsp Sugar

In a small bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes or until it begins to foam.

5

5 — 6 Cups Flour

6

2 1/2 tsp Salt

Place 2 cups of flour in the mixing bowl with salt. Stir in yeast mixture and begin to knead by hand. Add 1/2 cup of remaining flour at a time until the dough is smooth but not sticky.

7

1 tsp Olive Oil

Rub the ball with olive oil around the dough ball and let rest for 15 — 30 minutes. Turn the dough onto floured surface and divide in half. Roll one half in rectangle starting from the long side roll dough into a cylinder. Turn both ends in. Pinch the seams closed. Pound the edges and place on baking sheet (pinched side facing pan). Cut 3 cuts diagonal across the top of each loaf let rise 30 — 60 minutes. Preheat oven for 400∘ F. Bake for 17 — 20 minutes. Brush loaf with butter.

2

2 Pie Crust

3

5 Gala Apples

Slice the apples.

4

3 tbsp Lemon Juice

Put apple slices and lemon juice in a large bowl.

5

1/3 Cup Flour

6

1 dash Nutmeg

7

1/3 Cup Sugar

8

1/3 Cup Brown Sugar

9

1 tbsp Cinnamon

Add sugars, flour, and spices to bowl. Then toss to coat. Allow to the mixture to set a while so more juices are released. Sppon apple filling into chilled pie crust.

10

2 tbsp Butter

Cut butter into small pieces and sprinkle over apples. Roll out other pie crust, cut into strips, and apply to top. Press down on the edge of pie crusts to seal the two crusts together.

1 Egg

Orange Marmalade

____________________________________________________________________________________________

1

2

2 Medium Oranges

Use vegtable peeler to remove orange peels. Use knife to julienne cut rind to matchstick size pieces. Cut pith from orange. Cut oranges and add to a bowl.

3

2 Cups Water

4

2 Cups Sugar

5

1/2 Lemon’s Juice

6

Pinch Cinnamon

In sauce pan, add orange peels and water. Boil for 10 minutes and discard boiled water. Repeat boiling 3 times. In saucepan add boiled orange peels, orange, lemon juice, water, and sugar. Bring to a boil and simmer for 40 minutes until it has thickened. It should be thicker than water but not as thick as jam. Put in a jar and let it cool.

Sourdough Bread

____________________________________________________________________________________________

1

2

250 g Water

3

150 g Sourdough Discard

4

25 g Oil

Combine water, sourdough discard, and oil in a bowl and whisk.

5

500 g Flour

6

10 g Sea Salt

Add flour and salt to mixture. Squash mixture for flour to absorb water. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes. Let dough rise until it has doubled in size. Once it has risen, work dough and wait an hour for it to rise a second time. Preheat oven to 400∘ F. Bake in covered pot for 20 minutes. Then uncover the pot and bake for an additional 40 minutes. Let the bread cool before cutting.

Sourdough Tortillas

____________________________________________________________________________________________

1

2

3

1 Cup Sourdough Discard

4

3/4 Cup Water

5

1/4 Cup Oil

6

1 tsp Salt

Mix sourdough discard, water, oil, salt, and flour. knead for 5 minutes until dough becomes slightly elastic. Place dough in greased bowl, cover with tea towel and let sit for 12 — 24 hours. Then divide dough into 12 equal parts. Roll dough into balls, flatten into tortilla shape, and fry on iron skillet.
